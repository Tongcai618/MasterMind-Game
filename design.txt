Class MasterMind

Attributes:
    self.button_radius (int): the radius of the button (check_button, X_button).
    self.slot_radius (int): the radius of the big circles in the UI windows.
    self.reg_radius (int): the radius of the small circles in the UI window.
    self.row_interval (int): the vertical interval of each row, representing
                        different rounds in the game.
    self.colors (list): the constant list of colors for generating secret code
                    and for players to pick from.

    self.round (int)
