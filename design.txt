        Drawing methods draw basic shapes.
            draw_circle()   
            draw_solid_circle()
            draw_rectangle()
            draw_image()

    Generating Methods:
        Generating methods generates components of the UI window.
            generate_secret_code()
            generate_frame()
            generate_marbles()
            generate_hints()
            generate_selections()
            generate_X_button()
            generate_check_button()
            generate_quit_button()
            generate_arrow()
            generate_leaderboard()

    Reaction Methods:
            pop_up_window()
            read_leaderboard()
            write_text()

    Clicking Methods:
        Handling the action after clicking the button.

            click_selection_button()
                    |
                remove_solid_circle()
            
            click_X_button()

                recover_solid_circle()

            click_check_button()
            /       |            \ 
        win()       loss()   proceed_to_next_round()
                                /                 \
                        light_up_hints()      generate_selections()
                    
            click_quit_button()
                    |
                draw_image()
